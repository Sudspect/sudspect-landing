Veteran-Owned Startup Brings Florida's First AI-Powered Dog Wash Vending Machines to Apartment Communities

FORT LAUDERDALE, Fla., May 2026

Sudspect Dog Wash LLC Launches Zero-Cost, Revenue-Share Program for Florida HOAs and Apartment Properties

Sudspect Dog Wash LLC, a Florida-based, veteran-owned technology company, today announced the launch of its AI-powered autonomous dog wash vending machines across Florida apartment communities and HOA neighborhoods. The self-contained units allow residents to give their dogs a professional-grade wash — on their own schedule, steps from home — at a flat rate of $27.99 for small dogs and $34.99 for large dogs, with no subscription required.

The company's property partnership model carries zero upfront cost to building owners or associations. Sudspect provides, installs, and maintains all equipment and supplies, then pays participating properties a percentage of gross revenue — starting at 5% on a three-year agreement and scaling up to 20% on a ten-year term. The arrangement is designed to deliver a tangible amenity for residents while generating passive income for property managers without a capital investment.

"We built Sudspect for the communities that don't always get the best amenities — the working families in apartments, the neighborhoods where pet owners are making do with the closest groomer twenty minutes away. As a veteran, I understand what it means to serve something larger than yourself. This company is an extension of that mission. We're showing up for residents, for property owners, and for their dogs."

— Antwan Robinson, Founder and CEO, Sudspect Dog Wash LLC

Florida's market conditions make the timing compelling. The state is home to more than 21 million residents and ranks among the highest nationally for both pet ownership and apartment construction. With a wave of multifamily developments serving communities of 150 or more units, property managers face increasing pressure to differentiate their amenity packages. A built-in, always-available dog wash — requiring no staffing and no maintenance overhead — addresses a gap that traditional pet-friendly perks like dog runs and waste stations cannot fill.

Each Sudspect machine is engineered for convenience and hygiene. Units feature ground-level entry accessible to dogs up to 154 pounds, a fully automated 36-to-52 minute cycle that dispenses shampoo, conditioner, and blow-dry, and a triple disinfection protocol between every wash. The machines run on standard 120V power, eliminating the need for special electrical buildouts. The result is a unit that integrates cleanly into any property's existing infrastructure.

"Adding a dog wash to our amenity lineup was something residents had been requesting for years, but the cost and liability of a staffed facility made it a non-starter. A program where the operator handles everything — equipment, supplies, repairs — and we share in the upside is exactly the kind of partnership that makes sense for our team."

— Florida-based apartment community manager who evaluated the Sudspect platform

Sudspect is currently accepting partnership inquiries from Florida apartment complexes and HOA communities with 150 or more units. Interested property managers and association boards can learn more or request a placement consultation at sudspect.com.

About Sudspect Dog Wash LLC

Sudspect Dog Wash LLC is a Florida-based, veteran-owned company operating AI-powered autonomous dog wash vending machines inside apartment communities and HOA neighborhoods. Founded by U.S. Army veteran Antwan Robinson, Sudspect provides a zero-cost amenity solution for multifamily properties, pairing a convenient resident experience with a revenue-sharing model for property partners. Sudspect handles all equipment, installation, maintenance, and supplies. For more information, visit sudspect.com.
