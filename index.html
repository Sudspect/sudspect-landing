Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

What does it cost to install?

Nothing. Zero upfront cost to your property. Sudspect covers the full cost of the machine, installation, and all ongoing maintenance. You simply provide the space.

How does revenue sharing work?

You receive a percentage of revenue from every wash completed at your property. The exact split depends on your property's size and expected usage. Contact us for a custom proposal with projected monthly earnings.

How much space does the machine need?

The station requires approximately 4×4 feet of floor space, access to a standard water line, and a 220V outlet. Our team will assess your property and recommend the best placement.

Who handles maintenance and repairs?

We do, completely. Sudspect handles all routine maintenance, cleaning supplies, repairs, and service calls. Your staff is never involved. We operate on a 3-year placement agreement that covers everything.

Is the machine safe for all dog breeds and sizes?

Yes. The machine features gentle wash cycles, temperature-controlled water, and proper sizing for small to large breeds. A full sterilization cycle runs automatically after every wash.

What if a resident has trouble using it?

The 21.5" touchscreen walks residents through every step in plain language with multilingual support. QR code video tutorials can be posted near the machine, and our support line is always available.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

Our standard agreement is 3 years, which allows us to cover installation costs and provide you the best revenue split. We stand behind the product — we're here to build a long-term partnership.
